I created these icons for a CMS I'm writing called CMOGS, but I figure if CMOGS is going to be free, why can't these be?

<!-- more -->

So here they are, royalty-free! Use them as you wish, subtle shades of white and lack of white, blended together to give your site that semi-opaque feel, remnant of the no-hue inside each and every one of you.

There are about 30 icons, and I'm still making more as I find the time to.

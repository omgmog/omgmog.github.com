--- layout: post title: Google Plus userstyle for Reddit ---

I created a userstyle for Reddit to make it look more like Google+ and other redesigned Google sites.

To install you will need either Stylish or Stylebot for Google Chrome.

I’ve only tested this in Google Chrome, and it’s far from complete, but for now it functions nicely enough on subreddit pages and comment threads.

For the best experience, disable custom styles for subreddits.
